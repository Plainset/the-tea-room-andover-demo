What we serve

The Menu

A short, honest offer built around good tea, home-style lunches and a proper afternoon tea when you want to make an occasion of it.

Our offer

Light Refreshments

Served all day

A selection of light refreshments, available any time we're open — perfect for a quick stop on the High Street.

Lunch

Served from 12pm noon

Takeaway or eat in, from our lunch menu of an oven-baked jacket potato or a fresh baguette, with your choice of toppings.

Cream Tea

Served all day

For those wanting a little indulgence: a traditional cream tea of a freshly baked scone with jam and clotted cream, washed down with a pot of tea of your choice.

Afternoon Tea Experience

Bookings required

Every day is a special day, so don't wait for a birthday, anniversary or celebration to have an excuse to indulge in a traditional afternoon tea experience.

Tea & Cake

Sometimes all you need is a proper pot of tea and a slice of cake — no occasion required.

Menu items and prices can change with the seasons — for today's specials, or to check a booking for the Afternoon Tea Experience, give us a call or drop us an email before you visit.
